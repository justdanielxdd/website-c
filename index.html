About Us

We learn & have fun

Chan Tutoring Center was first established in 2018 by Ms. Chan in Mapleton, Brooklyn. Ms. Chan is a New York State licensed teacher certified in Childhood Education and Early Childhood Education with bilingual extension. Ms. Chan taught students at P.S 204 in Bensonhurst and P.S/I.S 282 in Park Slope. Our mission is to provide highly effective learning experiences to support students’ academic needs with rigorous instructions. We aim to inspire and motivate students to maximize their achievement along with the collaboration between educators and parents. We provide an after-school program, 1-on-1 tutoring, state-wide prep, Shsat & SAT Prep, kid coding class, and Chinese class.
